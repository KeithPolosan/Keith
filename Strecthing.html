Fitness Stretching

Benefits of Stretching

Stretching helps improve flexibility, increase range of motion, and reduce muscle tension.

Types of Stretching

  • Static Stretching
  • Dynamic Stretching
  • Ballistic Stretching
  • PNF Stretching

Stretching Tips

  1. Warm up before stretching.
  2. Hold each stretch for at least 20-30 seconds.
  3. Never stretch to the point of pain.
  4. Breathe deeply and consistently.
