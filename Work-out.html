Fitness Work-out

Crunches Exercise

Crunches are a classic abdominal exercise that targets the core. Here are the benefits:

  • Strengthens abdominal muscles
  • Improves core stability and posture
  • Enhances athletic performance
  • Helps define muscle tone (with proper diet)
  • Requires no equipment

Tip: Perform crunches with controlled form to avoid neck strain.
