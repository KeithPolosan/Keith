Fitness Budget Tips

Stay fit without breaking the bank

Top Tips

  • Use free workout videos: Platforms like YouTube offer high-quality, guided sessions.
  • Go for bodyweight exercises: Push-ups, squats, planks need no equipment.
  • Utilize local parks: Many parks have running paths and simple equipment.
  • Buy second-hand gear: Check local listings for affordable dumbbells or mats.
  • Plan your meals: Meal prepping reduces waste and improves nutrition.
