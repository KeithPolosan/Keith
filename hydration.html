Stay Hydrated. Stay Fit.

Water is essential to your fitness goals. Discover how hydration boosts performance and recovery.

Why Hydration Matters

Proper hydration improves energy levels, supports metabolism, and helps prevent injuries. Dehydration can lead to fatigue, cramps, and reduced performance.

Daily Water Intake

Aim to drink at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water per day. More may be needed if you’re active or in hot climates.

Pro Tips

  • Drink water before, during, and after workouts.
  • Use a hydration tracker app or bottle with markers.
  • Add electrolytes for intense training sessions.
